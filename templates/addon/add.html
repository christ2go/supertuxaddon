Add a new addon!

You can select between managed and classic mode. In managed mode, your don't have to worry about keeping the ".nfo"-file (it's the file, in which everything about your addon is stored), it is generated automatically. In classic mode, you have to supply a .nfo file with every version of your addon.

General information about your addon.

Your addon repos name will be: None
You can write a description of your addon in markdown format here. It will be shown on your addons webpage.
If you don't want to use managed mode, just deselect it and you're done!
In managed mode you have to select a license for your project, and have to add an author.
Possible licenses include GNU GPL, MIT license and many more. (Consult choosealicense if you don't know what to choose).

Last (but not least) your or the authors name (you should be the author, but just incase maybe others participated), is needed.

Your or your teams name. (This name will be displayed in game)
