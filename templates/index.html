Deploy your addon

Inorder to deploy your addon you first have to login with your github account (if you don't have one, you must create one). Then you can create your addon, you can also import it from the current addon-index, or from any github repository.
Once you added your addon, you can publish a new version, and therefore make it accessible. It's easy as cake.
